Who We Are

A development and acquisition company built around the work itself.

Black Jett Development, LLC is a real estate development and acquisition company focused on residential spec homes, small to medium multifamily projects, and development-driven property opportunities.

We evaluate land, infill sites, and residential assets with a practical focus on site feasibility, construction economics, and clear exit strategy. Our single-family work is focused on spec home development, not third-party custom home contracting. We build what the land allows and the market demands.

Whether executing a single-family spec build or evaluating a strategic acquisition, we evaluate each property through a builder's lens.
