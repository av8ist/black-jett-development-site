Black Jett Development  /  Multifamily

Multifamily.

We focus on the strategic development of small to medium multifamily assets, with a practical understanding of local geography.

We deliver residential communities built for longevity, stable occupancy, and manageable operational requirements.

Practical Infrastructure

We build for longevity. Our projects incorporate technical planning designed to support modern renters while protecting the physical asset over time.

  • Connectivity Integration

    Structured wiring designed into the building framework to ensure reliable networking capabilities required by today's residents.

  • Climate Management

    Smart thermostats optimize energy usage for the resident and assist in managing costs during unit turnovers.

  • Utility Efficiency

    Efficient water heating systems and water-conservation plumbing fixtures optimize ongoing operational costs.

Durable Finish Standard

Property value is maintained through practical, resilient design. Our units feature a durable finish package that stands up to turnover.

  • Resilient Surfaces

    Engineered solid surfaces provide a clean aesthetic without the recurring maintenance or resealing requirements of natural stone.

  • Hardware Selection

    Soft-close cabinetry hardware in kitchens and baths reduces impact-related wear and extends the usable life of installations.

  • Lighting & Maintenance

    Standardized LED lighting packages and practical architectural choices that limit ongoing maintenance and work orders.
