Acquisitions through a builder's lens.

Strategic evaluation of land, infill sites, and off-market residential assets.

Development-Driven

Identifying value through practical analysis.

Black Jett Development, LLC reviews land, residential properties, and off-market opportunities that may fit our development, redevelopment, resale, or long-term hold criteria.

Because we evaluate each property through a builder's lens, each opportunity is scrutinized based on location, condition, title, access, utilities, zoning, estimated construction cost, and exit potential before capital is committed.

Submit a property for review

Location & Condition

We assess the fundamental value of the physical property, its surrounding market dynamics, and current structural condition to determine feasibility.

Zoning & Utilities

A practical evaluation of title, access rights, current utility status, and municipal zoning allowances dictates our acquisition decisions.

Construction Economics

Every opportunity is evaluated against realistic estimated construction costs and modeled for a clear exit strategy.
